Introduction

Durban Connect is a digital platform started in 2024 to solve the problem of scattered, unreliable event information in Durban. It was inspired by personal experiences of missing events due to poor communication and unclear details.

The platform focuses on event listings so users can easily search by date, category, or location, view accurate information, and RSVP. It also helps organisers reach wider audiences beyond social media. With added community features for sharing plans with friends, Durban Connect aims to support local culture and small businesses, and create a more connected city.
